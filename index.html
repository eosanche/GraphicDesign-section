Where You At? Where You Going?

Jan. 2025.

This was a project created for one of the classes within the Global Business and Digital Arts program where it aimed to help me define and hone my sense of taste as a designer. The project required the use of Artifical generated content however, it depended on the designer on how much they would be willing to use it. I avoided to use too much Artifical generated content in my pieces however, I made sure these assets are blended well and are incorporated smoothly into each design. The generated pieces of content is the mountain and the abstract shape on the bottom corner of the first poster.

These projects were challenges towards my attention to detail and my ability to use past methods to create each design. The piece was created in illustrator and used the shape/pen tools along with the use of the image trace tool to create complex details of the design.
